About me

My name is Adinai, I am 22, I am from Kyrgyzstan, but for the third year I have been living in Korea and studying for a graphic designer. Was this idea easy for me? Fly to the country to fulfill a dream and fight misunderstandings every time. Definitely not, but believe me, it’s worth it! Like many, my illness called “Korea” began at school, when by chance on TV I saw multi-colored headgear of Asian guys who were singing about something in an abandoned amusement park. It turned out to be Big Bang, my first and big love!
